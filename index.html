Arsenal FC are a prestigous club in England and hold the record for the most FA cups at 14. Arsenal have won a league title 13 times the most notable being the 2003/04 PL season. They are the only team to have gone invincible in the modern Premier League, the closest other one was Liverpool being one win/draw off.
