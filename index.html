ARSENAL FOOTBALL CLUB

Arsenal FC are a prestigous club in England and hold the record for the most FA cups at 14. Arsenal have won a league title 13 times the most notable being the 2003/04 PL season. They are the only team to have gone invincible in the modern Premier League. They have won 13 Premier League trophies, 14 FA Cups, 2 league cups 16 FA Community Shields and 1 European Winners Cup. They play at the Emirates Stadium which can hold upto 60,704 Fans. The owner at the time (2021) is Stan Kroenke. They are close rivals to Chelsea and Spurs but nowadays they get beat by them normally.
